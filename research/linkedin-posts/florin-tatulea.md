## 1. A signal-based GTM play: vendor + contract-renewal timing
**Posted:** ~2026 · [permalink](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/florintatulea_sales-outbound-gtmengineer-ugcPost-7450969839407542272-bCsO)

> "This play is GOLD! It's historically converted at **5x standard outbound rates**." *(self-reported — see [`../other/fact-check.md`](../other/fact-check.md))*

Every Rev Ops / GTM Engineer / GTM leader should build an agent pushing this data to reps in real time. **Outbound is a game of precision — timing is everything,** especially for larger accounts (it's very hard to rip out an incumbent vendor mid-contract). He built it in GTM Studio; the agent tells you:
1. What **vendor** a prospect might have in place
2. When their **contract renewal** is likely coming up

From there:
1. **Cross-reference** against other signals (call-recording transcripts, web visits, etc.)
2. **Build initial messaging** so the rep does as little non-revenue work as possible
3. **Alert the rep at the perfect time**

*This is the clearest concrete example in the whole collection of "AI as a superpower" — the AI does the signal detection and prep; the human still runs the conversation.*
