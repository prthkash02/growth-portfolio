## 1. "Modern Prospecting": a full outbound motion (chapter breakdown)

8. **Testing, metrics & iteration**: the 4 metrics that matter; diagnose and fix without guessing.
9. **Automation & workflows**: core automations for scaling without adding headcount.

## 2. Your cold-call script matters less than you think: tone does
**Posted:** ~2026 · [permalink](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/outboundsales_sales-ugcPost-7457144942809911296-PfA_)

> "The only thing worth scripting is your opener; after that, it's just playing off what they say."

**Tone** is what opens a prospect up to telling you the truth instead of surface objections ("not interested," "I'm busy"). Done right, calls get you what emails never can: **second- and third-level objections.** People won't reply to an email explaining why they're not interested, on a call, they will. That's some of the best intel you can use to **sharpen your email copy.**

## 3. A hidden LinkedIn prospecting feature (engagement-based)
**Posted:** ~late 2025 · [permalink](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/outboundsales_sales-tangopartner-ugcPost-7411435410683424769-Feb4)

Find relevant posts in your niche, then prospect the people who engage with them. 4 steps:
1. Enter a relevant keyword in the search bar.
2. Filter by **"Posts."**
3. (Optional) Add filters, job title, location, industry.
4. Click into the **reactions** and find relevant prospects to reach out to.

(He also notes capturing repeatable workflows like this as step-by-step SOPs so they're easy to hand off to new hires/clients, an operations habit for scaling a prospecting team.)
